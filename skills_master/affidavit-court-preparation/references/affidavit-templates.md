# Affidavit Templates — Australian Courts

## WA Magistrates Court — General Form of Affidavit

The standard affidavit form for WA Magistrates Court is available at:
- [MCWA General Form of Affidavit (Word)](https://magistratescourt.wa.gov.au/_files/Form_Affidavit.doc)
- [MCWA General Form of Affidavit (PDF)](https://magistratescourt.wa.gov.au/_files/Form_Affidavit.pdf)

### Structure

```
IN THE MAGISTRATES COURT OF WESTERN AUSTRALIA
AT [LOCATION]

[FILE NO.]

BETWEEN:
    [APPLICANT/PLAINTIFF]
    [ROLE]

AND:
    [RESPONDENT/DEFENDANT]
    [ROLE]

AFFIDAVIT OF [DEPONENT NAME]

I, [full name], of [address], [occupation], make oath/affirmation and say:

1.  [Introductory facts — who I am, my connection to the matter]

2.  [First factual statement — one fact per paragraph]

3.  [Second factual statement]

    ...

[Last paragraph number].  [Prayer / conclusion / what I am asking the Court to do]

SWORN/AFFIRMED at [place]          )
in the State of Western Australia  )    _________________________
on [date]                          )    Signature of Deponent
                                   )
Before me:                         )
                                   )
_________________________          )
Signature of witness               )
[Justice of the Peace / Lawyer]    )
```

### Rules

- Each paragraph states ONE fact
- Numbered sequentially
- First person throughout
- Facts only — no argument, opinion, or submission
- Attachments referenced as "Annexure [letter]" marked by witness
- Date and location of swearing/affirming required
- Witness must be JP, lawyer, or other authorised person

## Affidavit for Variation/Cancellation of Restraining Order (s.45/46)

### Required Content

1. Identity of deponent
2. Connection to the restraining order (person bound / person protected)
3. Date order was made
4. Summary of the order's current conditions
5. Changed circumstances since the order was made (detailed, factual)
6. New evidence (if any) not available at original hearing
7. Why the variation/cancellation is sought
8. Annexures supporting the application

### Drafting Tips

- Use Form 12 (Application to Vary or Cancel) alongside this affidavit
- Focus on changes since the order, not relitigating the original hearing
- Provide documentary evidence of changed circumstances where possible
- Maintain neutral, factual tone throughout
- Do not attack the protected person's character

## FCFCOA Affidavit Requirements

### Federal Circuit and Family Court of Australia

- Follows Family Law Rules 2021 and Federal Circuit and Family Court of Australia (Family Law) Rules 2021
- Paragraphs numbered sequentially
- Annexures marked with letters (A, B, C...)
- Each annexure must have a cover page: "This is the annexure marked '[letter]' referred to in the affidavit of [name] sworn/affirmed on [date]"
- Witness must sign the annexure cover page
- Must comply with Practice Direction for the relevant registry

### Key Differences from WA State Courts

| Feature | WA Magistrates Court | FCFCOA |
|---------|---------------------|--------|
| Form | MCWA General Form of Affidavit | Court-specific template |
| Witness | JP or lawyer | JP, lawyer, or authorised person |
| Annexure marking | Letters | Letters |
| Annexure cover page | Yes | Yes (mandatory) |
| Filing | eLodgment or in person | Commonwealth Courts Portal |
