# Court Document Formats — WA & Federal

## WA Magistrates Court

### Filing Methods

1. **eLodgment** (eCourts Portal): www.ecourts.wa.gov.au
   - Registered users can file most civil and restraining order documents
   - Payment of fees online
   - Documents uploaded as PDF
   - Available for: civil claims, responses, affidavits, restraining order applications/variations

2. **In person**: At any Magistrates Court registry
   - Original signed documents required
   - Filing fees payable at registry

3. **Online Forms** (Legal service providers): mcwa online form system

### Document Requirements

- A4 paper size
- Single-sided printing for filed copies
- Black ink (unless colour evidence required)
- Signed originals for affidavits
- Sufficient copies for service on all parties

### Form Sources

All MCWA forms available at: https://magistratescourt.wa.gov.au/F/forms.aspx

Key forms:
- General Form of Affidavit: `Form_Affidavit.doc` / `.pdf`
- Form 12: Application to Vary or Cancel Restraining Order
- Bail Forms: 1, 5A, 5B, 7, 9, 14
- CPR Forms: 3 (Prosecution notice), 4 (Summons), 5 (Court hearing notice), 6 (Application in prosecution), 9 (Witness summons application), 10/11 (Witness summons)
- CJE Forms: 6, 7, 9, 12, 17, 19–22, 24, 28, 38, 38A, 38B, 41–43

## FCFCOA (Federal Circuit and Family Court of Australia)

### Filing Methods

- **Commonwealth Courts Portal**: www.comcourts.gov.au
- In person at FCFCOA registry (Perth: Commonwealth Law Courts Building, 1 Victoria Avenue)

### Document Requirements

- Follows Federal Circuit and Family Court of Australia (Family Law) Rules 2021
- Practice directions specific to WA registry
- Affidavits in prescribed form
- Annexures with mandatory cover pages

## WA District Court

### Filing Methods

- **eCourts Portal**: Available for some document types
- In person at District Court registry (State Law Building, Perth)

### Jurisdiction

- Civil: Up to $750,000
- Criminal: Indictable offences
- Appeals: From Magistrates Court

## General Formatting Rules (All WA Courts)

1. **Paper**: A4, white, 80gsm minimum
2. **Font**: 12pt, legible (Times New Roman, Arial, or similar)
3. **Margins**: 25mm minimum all sides
4. **Spacing**: 1.5 or double line spacing
5. **Paragraphs**: Numbered sequentially in affidavits and statements
6. **Headers**: Case name, file number, court on first page
7. **Page numbers**: Bottom of page, continuous
8. **Binding**: Top-left corner or left margin binding
9. **Copies**: Sufficient for court, all parties, and service
10. **Signatures**: Original ink signatures on filed copies
