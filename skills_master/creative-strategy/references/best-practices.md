# Creative Strategy Best Practices 2026

## Brand Development Framework

### Phase 1: Discovery & Research

**Research Methods:**
- Audience interviews (minimum 10 participants)
- Competitive analysis (5-7 direct competitors)
- Market trends assessment
- Stakeholder workshops

**Deliverables:**
- Audience personas (updated quarterly)
- Brand perception map
- Competitive positioning matrix
- Trend opportunity analysis

### Phase 2: Strategy & Positioning

**Positioning Statement Template:**
```
For [target audience]
Who [need/gap]
[Brand] is a [category]
That [key benefit]
Unlike [main competitor]
Because [reason to believe]
```

**Brand Pillars:**
1. Purpose - Why we exist
2. Vision - Where we're going
3. Mission - How we'll get there
4. Values - What we stand for

### Phase 3: Identity Development

**Visual Identity System:**
```
brand/
├── logo/
│   ├── primary.svg
│   ├── icon.svg
│   └── variations/
├── colors/
│   ├── primary.json
│   └── tokens.json
├── typography/
│   └── fonts.json
├── imagery/
│   ├── style-guide.md
│   └── samples/
└── motion/
    ├── principles.md
    └── animations/
```

### Phase 4: Implementation

**Rollout Checklist:**
- [ ] Update all digital assets
- [ ] Create brand guidelines site
- [ ] Train internal teams
- [ ] Update external communications
- [ ] Set up asset management
- [ ] Establish brand governance

## Audience Framework: AU/Western 20-35 (2026)

### Psychographic Updates

**Values (ranked):**
1. Authenticity over polish
2. Community belonging
3. Sustainability-conscious
4. Experience over ownership
5. Digital-first but human

**Communication Preferences:**
- Video-first content
- Interactive experiences
- Transparent communication
- Humor with substance
- No performative allyship

### Language Guidelines

**Do:**
- Conversational, direct
- Self-aware, slightly ironic
- Values-aligned messaging
- Emoji-literate (strategic use)

**Don't:**
- Corporate jargon
- Hustle culture language
- Generic "synergy" speak
- Performative inclusivity

## LGBTQ+ & Community-First Guidelines (2026)

## AI Integration in Creative (2026)

### Tools & Applications

| Tool | Use Case | Best For |
|------|----------|----------|
| Midjourney v7 | Concept art, mood boards | Rapid iteration |
| DALL-E 3 | Product visualization | E-commerce |
| Adobe FireFly | Brand assets | Production work |
| Runway | Video content | Social media |
| Suno | Audio branding | Sonic identity |

### AI Collaboration Workflow

1. **Briefing AI** with brand guidelines
2. **Generating variations** (10-20 options)
3. **Human curation** and refinement
4. **Final polish** with human touch
5. **Quality assurance** against brand standards

### AI-Generated Content Guidelines

- [ ] Review for brand alignment
- [ ] Check for bias/issues
- [ ] Ensure accessibility
- [ ] Add human touches
- [ ] Document AI usage
