# Influencer & Content Creator Marketing

## The Creator Economy Landscape

### Creator Tiers & Partnership Value

**Nano (1K-10K followers)**
- Highest engagement rates (4-8%)
- Hyper-niche communities
- Authentic, grassroots reach
- Cost-effective for testing
- Best for: Local campaigns, niche products, community building

**Micro (10K-100K followers)**
- Strong engagement (2-4%)
- Defined audience expertise
- Personal relationship with followers
- Scalable authenticity
- Best for: Product launches, sustained partnerships, UGC generation

**Mid-Tier (100K-500K followers)**
- Professional content quality
- Established brand collaboration experience
- Broader reach with maintained trust
- Category authority
- Best for: Awareness campaigns, brand positioning

**Macro (500K-1M followers)**
- Mass awareness capability
- High production value
- Celebrity-adjacent influence
- Media crossover potential
- Best for: Major launches, brand repositioning

**Mega (1M+ followers)**
- Cultural impact
- Global reach
- Premium pricing
- Lower engagement, higher visibility
- Best for: Brand prestige, cultural moments

## Creator Brand Development

### Personal Brand Architecture

**Brand Pillars (3-4 maximum)**
Content themes that define the creator's territory:
- Primary pillar: Core expertise/passion (60% content)
- Secondary pillar: Supporting interest (25% content)
- Personal pillar: Human connection moments (15% content)

**Signature Elements**
- Visual motifs (colours, filters, compositions)
- Verbal catchphrases or sign-offs
- Content formats they own
- Recurring segments or series

**Positioning Statement for Creators:**
```
[Creator name] helps [specific audience] 
achieve [outcome/feeling] through 
[content type/approach] that [unique differentiator].
```

### Content Pillars Framework

**Educational:** Teaching, tutorials, how-tos
**Entertaining:** Humour, trends, challenges
**Inspirational:** Motivation, transformation, aspiration
**Personal:** Behind-the-scenes, day-in-life, vulnerability
**Community:** Engagement-first, audience features, collabs

## Influencer Campaign Strategy

### Partnership Models

**Sponsored Content**
- Single post/video featuring brand
- Clear deliverables and messaging
- Usage rights negotiation critical

**Brand Ambassador**
- Long-term relationship (3-12 months)
- Multiple content pieces
- Exclusivity considerations
- Deeper brand integration

**Affiliate/Commission**
- Performance-based compensation
- Unique codes or links
- Lower upfront, higher potential

**Product Seeding**
- Gifting without obligation
- Organic mention potential
- Relationship building focus

**Co-Creation**
- Product development collaboration
- Limited edition releases
- Shared ownership/revenue

**Takeovers**
- Creator controls brand channels
- Fresh perspective for audience
- Cross-pollination of communities

### Campaign Brief for Creators

```
Brand Overview: [Who you are, values alignment]
Campaign Goal: [Specific objective]
Key Messages: [2-3 points to convey]
Creative Freedom: [Mandatories vs. flexible elements]
Content Format: [Platform, type, length]
Timeline: [Key dates, approval process]
Compensation: [Fee, product, affiliate structure]
Usage Rights: [Where content can be repurposed]
FTC/Disclosure: [Compliance requirements]
Success Metrics: [How you'll measure]
```

## Creator-Brand Fit Assessment

### Alignment Checklist
- [ ] Audience demographics match target
- [ ] Values alignment (check past content)
- [ ] Aesthetic compatibility
- [ ] Engagement quality (not just quantity)
- [ ] Past brand partnership performance
- [ ] Content authenticity indicators
- [ ] Community sentiment analysis

### Red Flags
- Engagement rate anomalies (bought followers)
- Controversy history without growth
- Over-saturated with sponsorships
- Misaligned past partnerships
- Poor disclosure practices

## UGC Strategy

### User-Generated Content Framework

**Earned UGC:** Organic customer content
- Monitor and request permission
- Showcase and celebrate
- Build community program

**Incentivised UGC:** Prompted creation
- Hashtag campaigns
- Contests and challenges
- Review generation

**Commissioned UGC:** Paid authentic-style content
- UGC creators (not influencers)
- Ad creative production
- Testimonial style content

### UGC Best Practices
- Always secure usage rights in writing
- Credit creators when showcasing
- Diversify creator representation
- Maintain authenticity—don't over-edit
- Build ongoing creator community
